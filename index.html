There are 3 types of groom you can opt for:

Wash & Blowdry

Wash & Blowdry w/ brush out of undercoat.

Mini-Groom:

Consists of a Wash & Blowdry w/ brush out of undercoat, Nail Trim, Eye Trim, Bum Trim & Perfume to finish.

Full Groom:

This consists of a Wash & Blowdry w/ brush out of undercoat, Nails trimmed & filed,
Ears Plucked & Cleaned, Anal Glands expelled, Hygiene Clip (Bum & Belly Shave),
Hair between Pads trimmed back and finally a full body / face cut & Style.
For Short Haired dogs such as Labrador, a complete brush out of dead undercoat is completed, which greatly reduces shedding!
